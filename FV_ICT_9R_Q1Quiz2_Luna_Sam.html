My favorite animated film "Moana" was released in 2016. The movie was about this adventurous girl who sailed the sea to save her people. This 16 year old teen is the daughter of the chief of her tribe. It was said that she came from the islands of Motunui. I bet you are aware of her companion, Maui. He is a demigod who guided Moana on her mission. The main message of the story is to follow your dreams and be who you truly are. Explore new things, be independent, and be courageous.
